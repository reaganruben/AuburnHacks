Panama Beach

Panama City, Florida

Mr. Coconut has rolled onto Panama City Beach, Florida! Located on the Gulf of Mexico, this beach is an All-American favorite with white sands and emerald waters. It attracts nearly 4.5 million tourists each year due to its beautiful beaches and famous entertainment destination, Pier Park.

Mr. Coconut made a friend along the way, Mr. Shrimp. After an exhausting time surfing through the crowded beach, they found themselves wandering towards the strip. The strong smells of sunscreen, fake tan, nail polish, and coronas chased them right into the Red Lobster. At first glance, it seemed like the perfect place for a crustacean and a stone fruit to relax for a bit.
